Description

Coordinate system of a model.

Coordinates after the projection transformation has been applied. The visible area here is [-1;+1]^3, plus some patting to maintain the aspect ratio.

Location_Screen

Pixel coordinates in the view. Range is [0;width)*[0;height)*[0;1].

World coordinates.
